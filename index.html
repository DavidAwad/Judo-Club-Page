The Judo Club

What you need to know

Can anyone show up?

Sure! We're big on newcomers, and you'll pick things up fast, within a few practices you'll be throwing us around in no time.

Always Practice

We practice at least three times a week, Every Wednesday and Friday from 6pm to 8pm and every Saturday from 12 to 2pm. Come join us!

Come to our Dojo

We practice in the College Avenue Gym Wrestling room, feel free to come, now matter who you are or what your skill level. If you want to learn, we want to have you.

About

Here's a little bit about us

  • 2010

    Our Humble Beginnings

    The first Rutgers Judo Club was founded by Vivek Singh. This really cool swag machine that is actually a black belt in Judo. He actually still comes to practices.

  • March 2011

    A Club is Born!

    We started to see some real growth! The club spread all through rutgers and we now have quite a few regular members! We started a community!

  • December 2012

    So we got to training

    The Rutgers Judo Club now competes at multiple events across the northeast! We've got all kinds of members , from very experienced to complete beginners. And we're here to support you.
